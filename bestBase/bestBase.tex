\begin{em}
\centerline{\small{}What might the ``best'' number system be?}
\end{em}

If you got to hit the reset button on all our conventions about
how we count, and were tasked with finding the BEST number system
for how we refer to and talk about integers in our day-to-day lives
do you think you'd pick decimal?

Let's ignore that the fact
that Sesame Street drilled into several generations of kids heads
that ``10'' means ten things.

So what criteria might we use to select the method by which we count
and give names to each individual integer?
